How to Make Spaghetti

Description

Everyone will love this cowboy spaghetti, a fun twist with bacon, cheese, and pasta in a spicy beef and tomato sauce.
The best part is that itâ€™s a one-dish meal, cooked in a cast iron skillet.

Ingredients

  • 6 strips bacon
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 8 ounces spaghetti noodles
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1 (4 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • 1/2 cup shredded sharp Cheddar cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the bacon in a large cast iron skillet over medium-high heat, for 7 minutes.
  2. Remove the bacon then cook the onions and the ground beef in the same skillet.
  3. Add the broth then break the spaghetti in half and add it to the skillet.
  4. Add the tomato sauce to the noodles and cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Scatter shredded cheese evenly over top and add the bacon.
