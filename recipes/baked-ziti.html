How to Make Baked Ziti

Description

This baked ziti is always a hit! A lady I worked with brought this in one day, and everyone loved it.
Now it is the favorite of all my dinner guests. I made this also without meat, and it's well received.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound dry ziti pasta
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 2 (26 ounce) jars spaghetti sauce
  • 6 ounces provolone cheese, sliced
  • 1 Â½ cups sour cream
  • 6 ounces mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the noodles.
  2. brown ground beef and onion in a large skillet over medium heat.
  3. Spread 1/2 of the ziti in the bottom of the prepared dish.
  4. Top with Provolone cheese.
  5. Top with sour cream.
  6. Add 1/2 of the meat sauce, remaining ziti, and mozzarella cheese.
  7. Top with grated Parmesan cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven until heated through and cheeses have melted.
You can also try: Lasagna, Tetrazzini, and Spaghetti.
